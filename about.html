About Me

I'm Isaac Saxonov, a highschooler who thought their ideas were good enough to be shared. I created PigeonHoled to communicate what I find interesting and what I feel like writing about, thus the content is highly variable. I built this entire site from scratch, which means I wrote everything, coded everything, and made all of the design choices myself.

You can expect writing about my philsophical opinions, concepts I find interesting, my takes on movies and books, lessons I've learned, things I'm doing, and experiences I've had.

If you have any questions for me, any rebuttals to my points, or if you want me to write about something, you can contact me here.
